Background

Kyn was founded in 2022 to radically change the people's perspective of what a neighbourhood could look like. Our inspiration came from looking at how neighbourhoods are currently like among the younger generation, needing a digital solution to allow people in the neighbourhood to connect with one another.

Our mission is to connect neighbourhoods together through providing a platform for people to create and join both community centre run events and also community-driven events.
